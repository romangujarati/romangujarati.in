About

Bridging creative vision and execution reality — turning ambitious ideas into productions that actually get made.

Khushaal Solanki, better known as Roman Gujarati (romangujarati), is a Mumbai-based Producer working across commercials, branded content, digital campaigns, music videos, testimonial content, UGC formats, and emerging Gen-AI storytelling. He specializes in transforming creative ideas into executable productions, balancing creative ambition with production realities.

Having grown through the ranks of advertising and content production, Khushaal has built his career around solving problems, managing complexity, and bringing together people, processes, and creativity. From pitch decks and budgets to shoot floors and final deliveries, he is passionate about building systems that help great stories get made.
